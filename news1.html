Elon Musk's Grok AI Confirms New Bitcoin ATH, While ChatGPT Denies It

Tech billionaire Elon Musk has published a tweet to praise his xAI’s invention Grok versus its rival – the brainchild of OpenAI, called ChatGPT.

While Grok mentioned Bitcoin breaking a new price record the other day, ChatGPT has rejected this important news for the financial and cryptocurrency sector.

Quoting a tweet posted by Twitter user “Dogecoin Designer” about the recently set new Bitcoin all-time high and the reaction of the Grok and ChatGPT chatbots to this significant event, Musk tweeted: “Real-time systems ftw.”

The initial message of the “Dogecoin Designer” is as follows: “Grok - Bitcoin just broke its all time high. ChatGPT - Bitcoin has not broken its all time high. Grok is the most real-time AI. ChatGPT failed, yet again.”
